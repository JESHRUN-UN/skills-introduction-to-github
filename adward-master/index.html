About This Course


At Fr. CRCE, students mentor school children through LLC and social initiatives. This course streamlines these efforts, awarding credits to participants while fostering peer-to-peer learning. By mentoring in relevant subjects, students share knowledge, build leadership skills, and connect science and technology with societal progress, contributing meaningfully to India’s growth.

Our Teachers

Meet Our Teachers Who Guided Us All Along......

Prof. Kranti Wagle


As the Coordinator of the School Mentoring Liberal Learning Course at Fr. CRCE, I take immense pride in witnessing our students mentor school children through engaging activities that ignite curiosity and innovation. This course is not just about teaching—it’s about empowering young minds, fostering creativity, problem-solving, and leadership.
Through hands-on sessions in web development, IoT, science experiments, and more, our students inspire schoolchildren to explore beyond textbooks and embrace practical learning. This initiative cultivates a spirit of collaboration, confidence, and technical excellence, ensuring a brighter future for both mentors and mentees.
As the Institute Innovation Council Convener, I firmly believe that mentorship is a two-way journey—it enriches both the learners and the mentors, shaping them into future leaders and innovators.
I invite more students and educators to join us in this transformative experience and contribute to building a generation of thinkers, creators, and problem-solvers!

Prof. Kranti Wagle
Computer Engineering Department
Fr. CRCE
